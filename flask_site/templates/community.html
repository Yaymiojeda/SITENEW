Where to go?
The Healthish community enjoys places that offer a variety of fresh produce and that help local businesses. Farmer's markets around New York City offer a farm to table experience, yet not everybody can afford $7 organic blueberries. It's not about where you go, but what can you do with what you have. Shop local, seasonal, and remember that 'good' is different for everybody.
