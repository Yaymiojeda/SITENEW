These are my classic banana-oats protein pancakes. Where do the protein come from? Mainly egg whites and 1/2 scoop of whey protein. “...we don’t care about the protein, what’s that purple thing on top?” I am glad you asked! I am a tahini addict, but it doesn’t go well with pancakes. I am a bit crazy, yet that’s just way too much. I grabbed a handful of fresh blueberries and put them on a small bowl with about 2 table spoons of water, heat them at for 30 seconds and I got a colorful syrup. Since I wanted this to be a nice-looking picture, I needed the natural blueberry syrup to be dense - creamy- and that’s when tahini (which is none other than grounded sesame seeds made as a sesame butter, but with a fancy name) come to play!
